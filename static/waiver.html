About Us

Your local outdoor adventure experts since 2018

Our Story

Born on the River

Riverview Adventure Company started with a simple idea: make it easy for everyone to enjoy the beautiful Wisconsin River. What began as a small tube rental operation has grown into a full-service outdoor recreation company.

Located in historic Sauk City, just 25 minutes from Madison, we're perfectly positioned on one of the most scenic stretches of the Lower Wisconsin State Riverway. Our team knows every sandbar, every bend, and every hidden gem along the river.

In 2023, we expanded to become an authorized Velotric e-bike dealer, bringing premium electric bikes to the Madison area. Whether you're floating down the river or cruising scenic trails, we're here to help you get outside and have fun.

Our Location

The Launch Pad

Our headquarters, affectionately known as "The Launch Pad," is your starting point for adventure. Located at 740 Water St in Sauk City, we offer:

  • Free parking for customers
  • Restrooms and changing areas
  • E-bike showroom and test rides
  • Bike repair shop
  • Snacks and drinks
What We Stand For

Our Values

Safety First

We monitor river conditions in real-time and only operate when it's safe. Your wellbeing is our top priority.

Community Love

We're proud to be part of the Sauk City community and support local events, schools, and organizations.

Quality Equipment

We invest in premium gear and maintain it meticulously. You deserve the best experience possible.

Fun for Everyone

From first-timers to seasoned adventurers, we create memorable experiences for all ages and skill levels.
