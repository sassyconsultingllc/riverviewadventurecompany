Stay Safe

River Safety Guidelines

Ideal Conditions (2,000-6,000 CFS)

Perfect for all activities. Gentle current, easy paddling, great for beginners and families.

Caution (6,000-10,000 CFS)

Faster current, experienced paddlers only. Tubing may be suspended. Call ahead to confirm.

Closed (Above 10,000 CFS)

River activities suspended for safety. Strong currents and debris hazards. Check back later.

Always Remember

  • Wear your life jacket at all times on the water
  • Stay hydrated and use sunscreen
  • Know your limits and the river conditions
  • Keep valuables in waterproof containers
