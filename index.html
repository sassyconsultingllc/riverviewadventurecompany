Where the river meets adventure, and memories are made.

001 · FLOAT

River Tubing

Drift down the Lower Wisconsin River on premium tubes. We shuttle you upstream to the VFW dam—you float back through sandbars, wildlife, and pure Wisconsin beauty. Bring coolers, bring friends.

002 · PADDLE

Kayak & Canoe

Take control of your adventure. Our kayaks and canoes put you in the captain's seat for exploring hidden channels, fishing spots, and secluded sandbars that tubers never see.

003 · EXPLORE

Bike the Trail

The Great Sauk Trail runs 10.5 miles of paved perfection along the river. Rent a bike from The Launch Pad and discover prairie views, river bluffs, and small-town Wisconsin charm.

Everything you need for a day on the water—or the trail.

Simple. Easy. Unforgettable.

No planning stress. Just show up and float.

01

Check In

Arrive at 740 Water Street in Sauk City. Grab your gear, sign a quick waiver, and load up on the shuttle. No reservation? No problem.

02

Adventure

We drop you upstream at the VFW dam. Float, paddle, or bike your way through Wisconsin's most beautiful river valley. Stop at sandbars. Take your time.

03

Return

Float right back to our location by sunset. Return your gear, and head home with a tan and stories to tell. Groups of 20+? Call ahead for VIP treatment.
